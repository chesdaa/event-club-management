What Information We Collect

The data we gather to provide you with our services

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name, email, student ID, faculty, year
  • Profile Data: Interests, club memberships, event registrations
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

How We Use Your Information

Why we need your data and what we do with it

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process event registrations and club memberships
  • Send you notifications about events and activities
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Provide customer support

How We Protect Your Data

Security measures we have in place

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password storage with hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Secure servers and databases

Your Rights & Control

What you can do with your data

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

Cookies & Tracking

How we use cookies on our website

We use cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our site
